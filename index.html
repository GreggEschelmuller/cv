About Me

Hi! I'm Gregg, I am a Phd student at the University of British Columbia. I am interested in how the nervous systems controls movement. I am working in the human neurophysiology lab and the perceptual motor dynamics lab. My research focuses on sensorimotor control and learning, specifically, I am interested in how the brain processes and learns from sensory information. One area of interest I have is how the brain builds predictions and using sesnory predictions erros to guide learning and sensorimotor adaptation. Check out my CV to learn more about my background and research!
