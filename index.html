About Me

Hi! My name is Gregg and I am a PhD student at the University of British Columbia. I am interested in how the nervous system controls movement. I am working in the human neurophysiology lab and the perceptual motor dynamics lab.

My research focuses on sensorimotor control and learning, specifically, I am interested in how the brain processes and learns from sensory information. Check out my CV to learn more about my background and research!
